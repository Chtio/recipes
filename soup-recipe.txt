Ingredients:
 - 1 large onion, diced
 - 2 tablespoons tomato paste
 - 1 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
 - Tortilla chips or strips

Directions:
  1. Heat the oil in a 6-quart saucepot over medium-high heat. Add the onion
  and peppers and cook for 2 minutes or until tender-crisp, stirring
  occasionally.
  2. Stir in the tomato paste, corn, beans, tomatoes, broth and chicken and
  heat to a boil. Reduce the heat to low. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring
  occasionally. Serve topped with crushed tortilla chips or strips, if desired.
